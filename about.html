Welcome to Crown Candy Kitchen!

Located in Old North St. Louis, Crown Candy Kitchen has been a staple of the city for over 100 years. Our old-fashioned restaurant will take you back in time where you can experience the joys of the jukebox, vintage collectables, and most importantly, iconic and amazing soda fountain food.

We do everything by hand - our ice cream is made fresh daily, our shakes are made from our own secret recipe, and our candies are made in store as well. Our sandwiches have even been featured as one of the Best Sandwiches in America . So come on down to our counter and let us give you the soda fountain experience of a life time.
